## Team Name Meaning

**DigiNeurons** = **Digi**tal + Neurons

The name connects our educational background in digital technologies with our project's domain — deep learning and neural networks. It tells judges immediately that we are students applying what we learned to build something meaningful.

---

## Project Name

**NeuroScope**

- **"Neuro"** = Neural network (the domain — deep learning)
- **"Scope"** = To inspect, examine, understand (the function)

"NeuroScope" is immediately descriptive to any ML/DL practitioner worldwide. It clearly communicates what the tool does — it lets you look inside neural networks and understand their architecture.
